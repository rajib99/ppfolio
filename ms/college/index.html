The higher education institutions listed below have made a commitment to employ the principles and standards of the College Cost Transparency (CCT) Initiative to help ensure that they communicate prospective and current student financial aid offers in a manner that is clear, accurate and helpful. By adhering to these principles and standards, these institutions aim to provide transparent and reliable information to students and their families, enabling them to make informed decisions regarding their financial obligations and educational choices. This commitment underscores their dedication to promoting fairness, accessibility, and clarity in the financial aid process, ultimately contributing to a more equitable higher education landscape.
